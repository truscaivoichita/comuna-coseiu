Accessibility Statement

This accessibility statement applies to this website.

Compliance Status

This website is partially compliant with WCAG 2.1 AA and the European standard EN 301 549 due to the non-compliances listed below.

Non-accessible Content

  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible.
  • Some images may lack alternative text.
  • Some form elements may need improved labeling.

Measures to Support Accessibility

  • Semantic HTML structure is used across pages.
  • Keyboard navigation support is implemented.
  • Visible focus indicators are provided.
  • Regular accessibility audits are conducted.

Feedback and Contact

Enforcement Procedure

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can contact the relevant national enforcement body in your country under EU Directive (EU) 2016/2102.

Technical Information

This website is designed to be compatible with modern browsers and assistive technologies such as screen readers.
